About Us

The Roadstar Trucking Company is an old-school LLC. helps companies attain operational excellence by providing transportation
solutions tailored for their individual needs. Our job is to provide the most cost-efficient transportation while offering the highest quality
service available. Our years of experience have taught us that “a one size fits all” type of transportation does not work in this complex
and ever changing industry. Companies need real-time information from their suppliers to their end-user, which helps them reduce
lead times and optimize their inventory while reducing transportation costs. The reason for our continued growth and success is due
to making our customers satisfaction our number one goal at Road Star.
